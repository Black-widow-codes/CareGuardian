# CareGuardian Business Case and Value Proposition

## Purpose

This document explains the value of CareGuardian to healthcare organizations, healthcare professionals, patients, and future stakeholders.

It answers the question:

Why should anyone adopt CareGuardian?

---

# Executive Summary

CareGuardian is a Patient Safety Intelligence Platform designed to reduce preventable harm caused by missing clinical information, communication failures, medication discrepancies, and incomplete transitions of care.

The first MVP focuses on discharge safety by helping healthcare professionals identify information gaps before patients leave the hospital.

CareGuardian acts as a safety layer that sits above existing healthcare systems and helps ensure critical information is complete, communicated, and acted upon.

---

# Problem

Healthcare organizations generate large amounts of clinical information.

However, patient harm often occurs because:

* Information is incomplete
* Information is delayed
* Information is overlooked
* Information is not communicated effectively
* Accountability is unclear

Examples include:

* Missing discharge instructions
* Missing follow-up appointments
* Pending test results without ownership
* Medication discrepancies
* Communication failures during transitions of care

These issues can contribute to:

* Patient harm
* Readmissions
* Delayed treatment
* Increased healthcare costs
* Staff frustration

---

# Why Existing Systems Are Not Enough

Electronic Health Records are excellent at storing information.

However, EHRs are not always designed to continuously verify:

* Information completeness
* Follow-up accountability
* Transition safety
* Communication quality

CareGuardian complements existing systems rather than replacing them.
