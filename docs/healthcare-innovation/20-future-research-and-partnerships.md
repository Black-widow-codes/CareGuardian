# Future Research and Partnerships

## Purpose

This document identifies future research opportunities, validation activities, strategic partnerships, and innovation ecosystems that may support the growth of CareGuardian.

The goal is to create a roadmap for future collaboration, pilot testing, research, and implementation.

---

# Research Priorities

## Research Area 1 – Clinical Information Continuity

Key Questions:

* What information is most commonly lost during transitions of care?
* Which information gaps create the greatest patient safety risks?
* How can information continuity be measured?

Potential Outcomes:

* Improved discharge workflows
* Reduced communication failures
* Improved patient safety

---

## Research Area 2 – Medication Safety

Key Questions:

* Which medication discrepancies occur most frequently?
* Which medication errors are most preventable?
* How can medication reconciliation be improved?

Potential Outcomes:

* Reduced medication-related harm
* Improved discharge quality

---

## Research Area 3 – Clinical Handoffs

Key Questions:

* What information is commonly omitted during handoffs?
* How effective are existing handoff tools?
* What causes communication failures?

Potential Outcomes:

* Better handoff processes
* Improved accountability

---

## Research Area 4 – Explainable AI in Healthcare

Key Questions:

* How can AI recommendations remain transparent?
* What level of explainability is required for clinician trust?
* How should healthcare AI support decision-making?

Potential Outcomes:

* Increased AI adoption
* Safer implementation of clinical AI

---

## Research Area 5 – Patient Safety Analytics

Key Questions:

* Can recurring safety patterns be identified?
* Which indicators predict patient safety risks?
* How can proactive interventions be supported?

Potential Outcomes:

* Earlier risk identification
* Improved safety monitoring

# Long-Term Vision

CareGuardian aims to evolve from a student-led innovation project into a validated Patient Safety Intelligence Platform capable of improving healthcare communication, accountability, and patient safety across multiple healthcare settings.

---

# Strategic Goal

Build a strong foundation of research, partnerships, validation, and user engagement before pursuing large-scale implementation.

The success of CareGuardian will depend not only on technology, but also on trust, usability, clinical relevance, and measurable improvements in patient safety.
